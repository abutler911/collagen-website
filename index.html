Why Do You Need Collagen?

Improve skin moisture, tone, and texture.*

Collagen is a vital protein that makes up around 25–35% of all protein in the human body. After age 30, collagen production declines, which can lead to saggy, dull, and wrinkled skin.

Collagen's benefits go well beyond the skin — it helps strengthen nails and hair, supports muscle recovery, cushions joints and ligaments, strengthens bones, and supports gut, heart, sleep, and brain health.

Alongside stress reduction, proper sleep, a nutritious diet, and a good skincare routine, a collagen supplement can help you look and feel your best from the inside out.
